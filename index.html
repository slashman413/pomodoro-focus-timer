About this free Pomodoro focus timer

The Pomodoro Technique breaks work into focused sprints separated by short breaks, helping you beat procrastination and avoid burnout. This free timer runs in your browser with customizable work and break lengths and optional desktop notifications.

How to use

  1. Set your work and break durations (the classic default is 25 / 5 minutes).
  2. Press start and focus on a single task until the timer rings.
  3. Take the short break, then begin the next sprint.
  4. After about four cycles, take a longer break.

Why use it

Time-boxing makes large tasks feel manageable and builds a healthy rhythm of focus and rest. The visual countdown and notifications keep you on track without installing an app or signing up.

FAQ

What is the ideal work/break length?

Classic Pomodoro is 25 minutes of work and a 5-minute break, but you can tune both to match your attention span.

Will it alert me if the tab is in the background?

Yes, if you allow browser notifications when prompted.

Does it remember my settings?

Your preferred durations are kept in your browser's local storage.
